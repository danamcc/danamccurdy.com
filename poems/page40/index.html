Beyond the world of discovery that music has been for me, there was always the bigger world, the universe of questions about what we’re a part of, how it works, and how to best fit into the larger whole.

My love of words, a fascination with scientific, philosophical, and spiritual inquiry, and a quiet determination to learn how to be a better human (and a happier man) have led me on a path of shaping my experience, breath by breath, into poems.

Here are some of them.

life love

Music

October 9, 2016

Music
is what the mind sounds like
when it isn’t thinking.

It is the yang
to the yin
of silence.

Money

October 9, 2016

In our minds,
money
is a form of energy.

We can trade it
for a bigger something,
a sexier something,
a more secure something,
a more respected something.

It becomes the air we breath,
the drug that sustains us,
the wall that protects us
from the fear
that we are not complete
as we are.

Living

October 9, 2016

Living
is allowing
life.

Trying
to survive
is not living.

The greatest joy
comes
with the least
struggle.

leaves in a stream

October 9, 2016

Our bodies sail through life
like leaves in a stream.

They float with it
because they are
not separate from it.

They are part of the stream,
but in a different form.

And who rides along,
seeing this flow,
often afraid
that we cannot control it?

What stream of thoughts
do we also ride on,
likewise powerless
to control them?

We are the pilots,
spirits riding energy,
but mostly we watch,
and, in moments
of epiphany,
we may nudge ourselves
into a different flow.

In this sea of uncertainty

October 9, 2016

In this sea of uncertainty,
we are only guaranteed
this moment.

To savor it,
with gratitude,
is to be fully alive.

Ignorance

October 9, 2016

Most ignorance
stems from the lack
of opportunity
or inspiration
to learn.

But surely,
it is the truly stupid
who cherish
their own ignorance,
protecting it,
as if it were
their own identity.

I am the witness

October 9, 2016

I am not my history,
I am the witness.

I am not the beauty I see,
I am the witness.

I am not what I create,
I am the witness.

I am all of these things,
and none of them.

I am the witness.

I am not my dreams,
and I am not my fears,
I am the witness.

Healing

October 9, 2016

Do not expect others
to heal your wounds;
they are just as wounded
as you are.

Better to show yourself
for who you are,
wounds and all,
and accept what comfort
there is
in simply sharing.

Fantasy

October 9, 2016

To delight in fantasy
is to give shape
to our imagination,
manifested in the world.

To savor reality
is to let the richness of the world
take shape in our minds,
infusing us.

To confuse the two
is to wander lost,
blind to the gifts
of each realm,
forever groping
for a world that isn’t.

Cynicism

October 9, 2016

﻿Cynicism
is just the mind’s defense,

a mechanism
to protect ourselves
from each other;

imagining differences
between us,

afraid to acknowledge
how we are the same.

It is the voice that says:
“I will not let THAT asshole know
that I am, perhaps,
an asshole, too!”.

But, in moments of grace,
there is another voice,
stripped of conceit,
that says:
“Yes, you are human,
as I am, too”.
