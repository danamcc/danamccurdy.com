Beyond the world of discovery that music has been for me, there was always the bigger world, the universe of questions about what we’re a part of, how it works, and how to best fit into the larger whole.

My love of words, a fascination with scientific, philosophical, and spiritual inquiry, and a quiet determination to learn how to be a better human (and a happier man) have led me on a path of shaping my experience, breath by breath, into poems.

Here are some of them.

life love

God

June 11, 2015

God
is the emergent intelligence
of the universe,
expressed everywhere,
and always.

It is the spirit
that permeates
everything,
but is,
mostly,
beyond our understanding.

It is our gift, however,
to experience it,
from time to time,
in glimpses
that transcend our minds.

Warning

June 8, 2015

Warning:

Language is a mind-altering substance.

The habit of thinking is an addiction.

Be careful what you believe -
what you think, may become real,
for better, or worse.

If I am not the person…

June 6, 2015

If I am not the person
I have imagined myself to be,
then I am not the failure
he may have judged himself to be.

I am like the open sky,
clouds passing endlessly,
infinite with possibility.

New affirmation

June 5, 2015

I
step
into
each
moment
with
awareness,
compassion,
and
readiness.

My Buddha

June 5, 2015

My own Buddha statue
is a wisp of smoke,
a glint of light,
shining
diamond-like,
in my mind’s eye,
from the center of my heart.

A Prayer

June 3, 2015

Universal energy
is flowing freely through me,
bringing light and healing
to my life
and the lives around me.

Life and Death

May 26, 2015

Life is not safe.

Only the dead
are guaranteed safety.

To avoid risk,
day in and day out,
Is to court death
before its time.

It is the fear of death
(which is the fear of life),
not death itself,
that causes suffering.

Healing

May 16, 2015

Immune system,
mind,
emotions,
and spirit
are inseparable.

Whatever we believe
will heal us,
with all our heart,
probably will.

God

May 9, 2015

God is the creative force of the universe.

When we allow that creative force
to flow through us,
God is with us.

Only when we block it,
not trusting our own divine nature,
does God disappear.

Building Worlds

May 9, 2015

Writers take their hopes and fears
and build worlds,
making them real
on the printed page.

And the rest of us?
We build worlds
with our hopes and fears
inside our minds,
and make them real.

Knowing this,
it is our choice
to build worlds
worth living in.
