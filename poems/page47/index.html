Beyond the world of discovery that music has been for me, there was always the bigger world, the universe of questions about what we’re a part of, how it works, and how to best fit into the larger whole.

My love of words, a fascination with scientific, philosophical, and spiritual inquiry, and a quiet determination to learn how to be a better human (and a happier man) have led me on a path of shaping my experience, breath by breath, into poems.

Here are some of them.

life love

meaning

October 22, 2015

Life has no meaning
except what we create
in our own thoughts.

A life well-lived
is made of thoughts
well-chosen.

Clouds and Sky

October 20, 2015

Thoughts and feelings
float past,
like clouds
in the sky.

I am not the clouds.

I am the sky.

Resistance

October 19, 2015

Resistance
is the barrier to acceptance.

Acceptance
is the gateway to love.

Magic

October 13, 2015

True magic
is hidden
everywhere
in the ordinary.

Seen clearly,
EVERYTHING
is extraordinary!

There is no myth
more magnificent
than What Is.

God ( a second reflection)

October 12, 2015

God is not a thing.

God is the essence
that permeates
ALL things.

Belief

October 11, 2015

I do not believe in God,
and I do not believe in air.

Yet,
I trust that there are things
I cannot see and hear
which shape the world around me.

Many teachers,
scientists and mystics,
have pointed the way,
and, at times,
my own senses
have confirmed their wisdom.

I do not believe in air,
yet that is the sea
I live and swim in.

So it is with Spirit,
which some call God,
always with us,
around us,
in us,
whatever it is called.

Happiness

October 9, 2015

Happiness
is a natural state.

It arises spontaneously
when we get out of our own way.

Everything Is a Dance

October 9, 2015

Everything dances.

Order dances with disorder,
Light dances with dark.

Form dances with emptiness,
Life dances with death.

Everything
is a dance.

the mind

October 6, 2015

The contents of the mind
are not the mind,
Just as clouds
Are not the sky.

Our thoughts
will float freely
through our awareness,
when we let them go,
drifting,
like clouds,
evanescent
against an endless blue sky.

who I am not

October 5, 2015

I am not
who I think I am
(that is only thinking).

I am not
the dream of my life -
I am the dreamer.
