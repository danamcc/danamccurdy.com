My Heart’s Eye

July 4, 2010 love

In my mind’s eye, I see you sleeping,
Nestled with the little cloth creatures
Who befriend you each night.

In my heart’s eye, I slip softly
Into your room,
Watching you, peaceful.

In my heart and mind,
I feel you next to me,
Breathing, alive.
