a place of calm and peace

October 27, 2015 life

here is a place
of calm
and peace
within us.

We need not
FEEL calm
to get there.

We need only
to be the observer
of whatever feelings
come and go.

Standing in that space,
we have all the calm,
and all the peace,
we need.
