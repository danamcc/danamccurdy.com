God

June 11, 2015 life

God
is the emergent intelligence
of the universe,
expressed everywhere,
and always.

It is the spirit
that permeates
everything,
but is,
mostly,
beyond our understanding.

It is our gift, however,
to experience it,
from time to time,
in glimpses
that transcend our minds.
