Thoughts

May 6, 2015 life

When you have
a conversation
with yourself,
who is talking
to whom?

Perhaps,
there is no one there,
only thoughts,
thinking,
and passing on.

You are not
your conversation,
but the One
who listens.

You are not
your experience,
but the Observer
of all you experience.
