In the Dream

November 5, 2015 life

When we seem to find
The person of our dreams,
We may see the dream,
Not the person.

Living in the dream,
We stand separate,
Isolated in our wants.

And,
When the dream dissolves,
We stand face to face,
Being to being.

Then,
The door opens,
Or it closes.
