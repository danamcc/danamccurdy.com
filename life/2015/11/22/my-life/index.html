my life

November 22, 2015 life

The story of my life
is not my life.

The drama of your story
is not your essence.

The morals of our stories
are not our truths.

When we look at our story,
and what is beyond it,
and see ourselves
outside it,
looking,
that is our life,
our essence,
our truth.
