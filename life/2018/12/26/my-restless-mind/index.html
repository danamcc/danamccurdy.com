my restless mind

December 26, 2018 life

Sometimes I catch myself
doubting myself,
and I have to ask myself,
“Who are all these selves?”

And sometimes I see
that only the one
who sees all this
is really me,
and all the rest
are merely dreams
and imaginings
of my restless mind.
