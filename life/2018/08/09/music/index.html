Music

August 9, 2018 life

Music is the universal language.

It speaks directly
to the heart and soul
of all that lives,
all cultures,
all species.

It brings comfort
to the savage beast,
even to the uneasy plant,
reminding everything alive
that we are not separate,
that we are,
in our essence,
all the same.
