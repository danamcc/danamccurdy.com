Outside

April 2, 2018 life

When I step outside
of my own mind,
out of the stream
of my jostling thoughts,
I find a realm of peacefulness,
already there,
where I am.

When I look outside
my own identity,
with all its little problems
and inadequacies,
I find a spaciousness
where it is enough to be
what I already am.
