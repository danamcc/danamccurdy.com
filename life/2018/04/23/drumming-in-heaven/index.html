Drumming in Heaven

April 23, 2018 life

It is easy to forget
we are a village
until we come together
to celebrate
what we love,
and what we are.

Through this ritual of drumming,
we become family again,
rediscovering what binds us
and defines us
as a community,
as we find ourselves,
once again,
in this Heaven
on earth.
