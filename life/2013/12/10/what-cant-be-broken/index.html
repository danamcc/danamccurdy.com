What Can’t Be Broken

December 10, 2013 life

When something feels broken,
sometimes it’s time to give up
trying to fix it,
holding it together,
and just let it fall apart.

Then, amongst its pieces,
we may find its essence,
the part that can’t be broken.

Then, perhaps,
something new
can grow.
