A Reflection

October 3, 2013 life

Human beings are,
for the most part,
unreliable.

Pinning one’s hopes
on the actions of another
is almost always a mistake.

The one relationship
we can and must rely on
is with ourself.

All the nurturing
and guidance
we really need
is within.

What we see in others
is simply a mirror
of what is in ourselves.
