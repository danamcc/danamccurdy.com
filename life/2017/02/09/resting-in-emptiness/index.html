Resting In Emptiness

February 9, 2017 life

I have been living in a dream
where I am always on the verge
of being right
or being wrong,
walking the razor’s edge
of triumph,
or disaster.

And then,
as I awaken,
it all blows away,
like so much smoke,
and I am resting,
comfortably,
in emptiness.
