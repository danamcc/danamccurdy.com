Clouds and sky

January 9, 2019 life

The clouds
are not the sky,
and my thoughts
are not the world.

When I get lost
inside my thoughts,
my world
is only clouds.

When I look outside them,
I am what looks at them,
then I see the sky,
and I AM the sky.
