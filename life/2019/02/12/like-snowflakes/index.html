Like Snowflakes

February 12, 2019 life

We are like snowflakes,
all made of snow,
all the same stuff,
expressing itself
in myriad ways.

We are permutations
of one great pattern,
simple and sacred,
repeating and mutating,
in endless variation.

We are one great spirit,
refracted through a million lenses.

We are each unique,
and each the same.
