most of our moments

June 11, 2020 life

In most of our moments,
the fear we feel
is pain we imagine
in the future.

And the sadness we feel
is pain remembered
in our past.

Without the mind
reminding us
of past or future,
there is only the present,
where, in most of our moments,
there is no pain
to be found.
