Moments: 2 Thoughts

January 1, 2014 life

Afloat in a river of moments,
Thoughts, opinions, judgements
Drift by.
What if this river ran clear?
What a trip that would be!

In each perfect moment,
A defect can be found.
Waiting to be fixed,
A lifetime passes by.
