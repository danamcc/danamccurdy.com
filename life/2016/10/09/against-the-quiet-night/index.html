Against the quiet night

October 9, 2016 life

Against the quiet night,
a flashing light
reminds me,
that somewhere,
there is always
someone suffering.

Though my eyes
may not see them,
may my heart stay open,
tender with this poignant truth,
that there is always
someone deserving
of compassion.
