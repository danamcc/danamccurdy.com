I am the witness

October 9, 2016 life

I am not my history,
I am the witness.

I am not the beauty I see,
I am the witness.

I am not what I create,
I am the witness.

I am all of these things,
and none of them.

I am the witness.

I am not my dreams,
and I am not my fears,
I am the witness.
