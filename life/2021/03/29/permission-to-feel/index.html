Permission To Feel

March 29, 2021 life

I give myself permission
to feel everything
without thinking.

I give myself permission
to see
without thinking.

I give myself permission
to hear
without thinking.

I give myself permission
to know
without thinking.

I give myself permission
to BE
without thinking.
