Letting Go

November 21, 2021 life

In this moment,
I don’t need a reason
to do whatever I do.

A reason
is only an excuse,
an attempt to explain
what is unexplainable.

In this moment,
I give myself over
to be a willing vessel
for whatever Life,
moving through me,chooses
for me to be or do.
