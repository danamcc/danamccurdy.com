What You Can Do

December 25, 2023 life

While cleaning out the cat box and repairing a urine-stained part of its enclosure, this flash of insight dawned:

The “best you can do”,
at any given moment,
is most likely just the best
you were WILLING to do,
in that particular moment.

Look past your willingness,
in that particular moment,
past your comfort,
in that moment,
and discover the power
of what you actually can do.
