In this moment,
I don't need a reason 
to do whatever I do.

A reason 
is only an excuse,
an attempt to explain 
what is unexplainable.

In this moment,
I give myself over 
to be a willing vessel
for whatever Life,
moving through me,chooses 
for me to be or do.
