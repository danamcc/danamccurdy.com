title: Letting Go
---
From this moment on,
I no longer need a reason 
to do what I do.

A reason 
is just an excuse,
an attempt to explain 
what is unexplainable.

From this moment on,
I will get out of my own way,
and be a willing vessel
for whatever Life,
moving through me,
chooses 
for me to do
or be.
